# Intro to Chemistry Remote Materials Pack

This packet supplies the card sets, data tables, and scenario lists referenced by the Intro to Chemistry course. All work can be completed with a browser, notes, paper, and shared-screen discussion. No household chemicals, heat, glassware, tasting, mixing, or outdoor collection is required.

## Ion and Formula Cards

Use these ions to build neutral ionic formulas by balancing total positive and negative charge.

| Positive Ion | Charge | Negative Ion | Charge |
| --- | ---: | --- | ---: |
| Na | +1 | Cl | -1 |
| K | +1 | F | -1 |
| Mg | +2 | O | -2 |
| Ca | +2 | Br | -1 |
| Al | +3 | O | -2 |
| NH4 | +1 | NO3 | -1 |

Example: `Mg2+` needs two `Cl-` ions, so the neutral formula is `MgCl2`.

| Element | Period | Group | Atomic Radius Trend | Electronegativity Trend |
| --- | ---: | ---: | --- | --- |
| Li | 2 | 1 | larger than Be | low |
| Be | 2 | 2 | smaller than Li | higher than Li |

| F | 2 | 17 | small | very high |
| Na | 3 | 1 | larger than Li | low |

**Use:** Across a period, radius generally decreases and attraction for electrons generally increases. Down a group, radius generally increases.

## Molar Mass and Stoichiometry Practice Set

Use approximate atomic masses: H = 1, C = 12, O = 16, Na = 23, Cl = 35.5, Ca = 40.

1. Find molar mass for `H2O`.
2. Find molar mass for `CO2`.
3. Find molar mass for `NaCl`.
4. Find molar mass for `CaCO3`.
5. Convert 36 g of `H2O` to moles.
6. For `2H2 + O2 -> 2H2O`, identify the mole ratio from oxygen to water.
7. For `CH4 + 2O2 -> CO2 + 2H2O`, identify the mole ratio from methane to water.

## Stoichiometry Error Cases
